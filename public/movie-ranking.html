The Best Movies (According to Pastel)

My top 4 movies of all time

Udaan (2010)

⭐⭐⭐⭐

A poignant, coming of age story of a boy stuck with an abusive father.

A Taxi Driver (2017)

⭐⭐⭐⭐

In 1980, a foreign journalist hires a down-on-his-luck taxi driver to take him to Gwangju, South Korea. They soon arrive to find a city under siege by student protesters and the military.

Tokyo Godfathers (2003)

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Middle-aged alcoholic Gin (Darren Pleavin), teenage runaway Miyuki (Candice Moore) and former drag queen Hana (Myrta Dangelo) are a trio of homeless people surviving as a makeshift family on the streets of Tokyo.

Only Yesterday (1991)

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Unmarried career woman Taeko Okajima (Miki Imai) takes her first extended trip outside her native Tokyo when she travels to rural Yamagata to visit her sister's family during the annual safflower harvest.
